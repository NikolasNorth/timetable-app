Privacy Policy

We collect the following information from our users:

  • Name
  • Email

We collect this information to create user accounts which permit special functionality on our website (e.g. creating schedules, reviewing courses.

Upon creating an account, your name and email will be stored in a database on our server. Your information will not be sold to third-party companies, and your information will be used for identification purposes only (e.g. logging-in, displaying author of reviews, schedules, etc.). Note, this means your name will be publicly visible to any user of this website. However, your email will remain private to all users (with exception to your own account and administrators).

In the event of a takeover or dissolution, we will provide an option to remove access to your name and email by deleting this information from database on our servers. In any case, a new privacy policy will be drafted in this situation and an option to opt-in or opt-out will be given.
